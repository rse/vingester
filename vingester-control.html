Vingester (Video ingester) is a small, Open Source licensed, and Electron-based desktop application for use under Windows, macOS or Linux to run multiple Chromium-based Web browser instances and ingesting their rendered Web Contents as screen/window-captured, NDI®-multicasted or FFmpeg-based video streams for further use in local or remote video mixing applications or for local recording.

Although not technically tied to it, the primary use-case of Vingester is to ingest the individual meeting participants of the awesome WebRTC-based VDO.Ninja solution as individual NDI-based video-streams into the mixing application OBS Studio in order to reduce performance drains within OBS Studio. The VDO.Ninja URLs of the ingested video-streams can be generated best with the opinionated companion tool VDO.Ninja Trampoline.
